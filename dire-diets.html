A major expansion is an expansion that adds new mechanics modifying the fundamental rules of the game.

Dire Diets

The major expansion "Dire Diets" adds a new category of card : food, with seven cards added.

Use foods that are as varied as they are flexible!

“After the effort, the comfort ; nothing beats a meal by the fire alongside your pets !”
Each food has a unique and simple effect.
Seven playable foods are available to players.

Bread

Cheese

Fish

Fruit

Fungus

Meat

Water

After the end of the first round, each player creates a personal food pile with the seven cards available.
During the combat phase, each player can only use the first food revealed from their pile during their turn.
At the beginning of each round, regardless of whether the card is used or not, it is placed in a discard pile specific to each player. Once the pile is empty, the discard pile is shuffled and placed face up.
