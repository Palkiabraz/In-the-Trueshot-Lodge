Tame pets with unique effects !

“Pets are your animal allies from Azeroth and beyond, fighting for you to the death.”

A pet has one of three available types : Ferocious, Cunning or Tenacious. Each type is advantaged over another type, removing an additional Health when the pet attacks.

A pet has a rarity among two available : Common or Exotic. Common pets are available in two copies while exotic pets are only available in one copy.

A pet has an Attack value and a Health value as well as a unique effect.

Purchase weapons as formidable as they are varied !

“The weapon is your only equipment available during this struggle of power.”

A weapon has a unique effect and an Attack value and a Cost value.

Each player has five Coins at their disposal to purchase a weapon. When the weapon is purchased, the player spends a number of Coins equivalent to the Cost of the weapon. However, if the weapon is not suitable for the player, they have the possibility to sell it and receive back the value of the spent Coins, minus one Coin.

Cast powerful spells capable of turning the tide of the game !

“Spells are single-use abilities that provide a strategic advantage over others.”

A spell has a unique effect and a name.

Indeed, unlike other cards, the name of a spell is important. Some pets interact with specific spells, enhancing them significantly.

Spells with special names are the Aspect, Arrow, Bite, Sting, and Shot spells.
