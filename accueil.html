Welcome in the Trueshot Lodge !

"You have just entered the sacred domain of all hunters, hidden among the highest snow-capped peaks of Highmountain. This domain gathers the greatest hunters Azeroth has ever known and is governed by the Unseen Path, an order bringing together the finest hunters among them.

Within these halls, only one rule matters: survival of the fittest !"

In the Trueshot Lodge is a competitive turn-based board game incorporating elements of chance and strategy.

Tame pets with unique effects, ranging from a loyal Hound to an impressive Hydra. Purchase weapons as formidable as they are varied, such as crossbows, hunting spears, or rifles. Cast powerful spells capable of turning the tide of the game, including shots, traps, and more.

Deploy your entire arsenal, gathered progressively throughout the match, to defeat your opponents and claim victory !

In the Trueshot Lodge is available for free in printable and digital versions on the Steam Workshop of Tabletop Simulator.
